High-Frequency English Verbs

These verbs appear everywhere in English. Master these and everything becomes easier.

  • get – receive, understand
  • go – move, leave
  • make – create
  • do – perform
  • have – own, experience

Example: “I get it now.” “I have time today.”

Everyday Phrases

UK: I’m knackered → US: I’m exhausted

UK: Fancy a coffee? → US: Want some coffee?

UK: I’ll give you a ring → US: I’ll call you
